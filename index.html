Our Vision And Mission

VISION

The vision of MM is “To bring hope and healing to the unreached people groups among the people of Greater Kapoeta, and other unreached communities of East Africa, transforming their lives holistically with Christ at the centre”.

MISSION

Medical Missions will aim at training and transforming the unreached communities in sub-Saharan East African nations holistically through provision of subsidised medical services and mobile surgeries, impacting them with the true gospel of Jesus Christ starting with the ones in greatest need. We would see this as a partnership with the communities and their leaders networking together with government services, the churches, medics from East Africa and medics doing short term medical missions with us from developed countries. Underpinning everything would be Jesus’ prayer in Matthew 6:9,10.

Objectives And Activities of MM

Health and Family Welfare

We encourage and promote Nutrition on Clinical Nutrition; through Integrated Management of Acute Malnutrition (IMAM), Institution Feeding Programs (IFP), Establishment of Stabilization Centers ((SC) to promote medical management of Severe acute Malnutrition and provision of nutrition supplements to pregnant women, mother and infants and Enhanced Nutrition Referral Pathways ,Community Nutrition; through Community Management of Acute Nutrition (CMAN), Blanket Supplementary Feeding Programme (BSFP),Targeted Supplementary Feeding Programme (TSFP), Maternal Infant and Young Child Nutrition; and Family Planning (MIYCN-FP), Food for Assets (FFA), Nutrition in Education School Meals (FFE). Nutrition in Emergencies; Nutrition Needs Assessment, General Food Distribution and Conditional Food Distribution

Evangelism And Church Outreach

When providing medical services, we share the gospel of Jesus Christ, letting individuals know that it’s Jesus who heals. Our priority is spiritual healing through Christ. To provide Counselling, trauma healing, reconciliation and forgiveness amongst communities. To support, partner with, renovate and develop churches across South Sudan.

Education And Good Traditional Cultural Practices

To educate and promote good cultural practices among the special groups like widows, children, orphans, youth and the disabled for sustainable good cultural practices and history. To advocate and campaign for Girl Child Education and against Gender Based Violence (GBV)through community awareness in diverse ethnic background. To advocate against bad and repugnant traditional, cultural practices, like early girl child marriage, girls or women genital mutilation, child sacrifice, etc. within South Sudan. Promote adult literacy in rural areas.

Agriculture And Livelihood

Food security and a balanced healthy diet are key to developing healthy communities. As such a holistic approach will incorporate education in areas of diet, nutrition, food supply planning and providing a healthy community environment. To promote modernized domestic and commercial agricultural activities as strategic economic empowerment for income generation activity for self-reliant strategy in South Sudan. Encourage economic empowerment and transformation amongst, children, women, youths and the elderly through sustainable counseling, guidance, vocational trainings and good decision-making skills. To promote capacity of the local communities through education, leadership Skills with the purpose of preparing communities/citizens self-reliance for better livelihood. To strengthen the capacity of communities to cope with and manage impending disasters in all aspects of life. To promote the development of a sustainable healthy environment planning for future generations in Agro-forestry decisions.

Protection And Humaniterian Aid

Focus on key childhood survival strategies (GOBI-FFF). To rescue and protect marginalized and vulnerable children, women, youths and the elderly people through creation and establishment rehabilitation/reformatory centers, and provision of shelters. To protect and improve the dignity of the most vulnerable populations of the IDPs, Women, children and the marginalized groups. Integrate, reintegrate and rehabilitate the Communities in South Sudan. To establish programs for peaceful conflict resolution/reconciliation, human rights and rule of law for peace building. Participate in Relief and Rehabilitation projects to support the needy communities.

Other Auxilary Objectives/Activities

To work together with other organizations with similar objectives. To apply to any government authority, Non-governmental organization (NGOs) both local and international, corporate entities or person and to accept grant of many or any other support with the view of promoting the any objectives or activities of the organization. To do any other thing which is incidental to consequential upon the objectives of MM
